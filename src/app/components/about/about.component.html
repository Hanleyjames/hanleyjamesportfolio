Introducing... Me?

Hey there! I am a nomad, born in New York, raised in Florida and recently relocated to Washington State. I spent 90 percent of my life in schooling and reading books. I used to be interested in Political Economy because the notion of separating Economics from Politics is shortsighted.

I have an A.A., in Human Services, a B.S., in Political Science, with a focus on Political Theory, and am one class away from finishing a second B.A, in Philosophy. My ideological Coordinates are a focus in Continental Philosophy with Kant, Marx, Lacan, and Deleuze. I don't read as much as I used to, but then again I needed a job that did not let me die of poverty. It turns out having disabilities and doing manual labor jobs can be quite detrimental.

As of now, my primary hobbies are Bouldering, sleeping, cooking and working on programming projects for people with physical disabilities. I don't care about much else.
